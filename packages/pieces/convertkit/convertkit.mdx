To obtain an API key on ConvertKit, you can follow these steps:

- Log in to your ConvertKit account.
- Click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu.
- In the left-hand menu, click on "Advanced".
- Copy API Secret
